Picture Show produced, directed, shot and edited the original content series 'One Track Mind' for the A.V. Club. Sponsored by Jameson Irish Whiskey, each episode features an A.V. Club writer journeying to a musician's hometown to the learn the inspiration for one of the artist's best-known songs. Each episode then concludes with a stripped-down, intimate live performance of that composition. Participants include St. Vincent, Bob Mould, Ben Folds and Jenn Wasner of Wye Oak.
