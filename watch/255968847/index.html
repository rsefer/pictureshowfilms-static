i Providence College :: Divine Providence

Close Video

Providence College celebrated its 100th anniversary in 2016, and Picture Show was part of the creative team in the campaign to mark the school's first century. Picture Show shot, directed and edited pieces for broadcast, digital and theatrical presentation, chronicling the leadership, academic excellence and community built on faith that has exemplified the Providence College experience through the decades. "Divine Providence" is one of the :30 broadcast spots included in the campaign, and features a narrative driven by men's head basketball coach Ed Cooley, who offers, in his own words, the opportunity that the sacred tradition of Providence College offers for those who heed the call.
