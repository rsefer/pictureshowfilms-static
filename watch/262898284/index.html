The AV Club Hosted by John Teti is a Chicago-based television series featuring the irreverent editorial brains behind the taste-making online entertainment website, The AV Club. Picture Show talent served as Executive Producer, Producer and Director for the series, which used a blend of satire, unscripted feature pieces and talk show commentary to lend a distinct perspective on movies, music, television, and culture.
