Picture Show played a vital role in developing the voice and identity of Small Forces, an organization that gifts pro bono media services to small, grassroots non-profits and worthy individuals. Picture Show's unscripted cinematic documentary storytelling is the engine that powers the mission of the team of strategists and creatives at Small Forces, who use Picture Show's work to create both a measurable positive impact for Small Forces' partner organizations and positive ROI for Small Forces' benefactors.
