A Day Made Better

OfficeMax

OfficeMax approached Picture Show  about making a video for their award-winning cause marketing program - "A Day Made Better." This video follows the story of two teachers, chronicling their dedication to teaching and telling the story of one company who surprises them with over $1,000 worth of essential supplies that, because of a insufficient budgets, they are usually forced to spend their own money on.
